The customed keys have certificate expiration over 30 years


Android App Debug Key
=================
keystore=debug.keystore
keystore_password=android
keystore_alias=androiddebugkey


Android App Release Key
===================
keystore=android-release-key.keystore
keyalias=releasekey
keyalias_password=BSMP@ssw0rd
keystore_password=BSMP@ssw0rd
